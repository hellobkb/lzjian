Company Introduction

Established in 2011, we specialize in producing carton machinery. With 10 million registered capitals, our company become a modern shareholding company. Engaged in carton equipment manufacturing and carton production over twenty years, the shareholders are all very familiar with whole carton industrial chain including the consumables purchasing, equipment configuration, carton manufacturing and the after-sale services.

We are mainly managing all kinds of consumables and spare parts for cartons, cardboards and their making machines as well their auxiliary machines. Meanwhile, we also provide the trainings for printer plate making, knife mould making and other corresponding management software.

Our company has successfully exported our products to East Europe, Australia, Russia, Africa, Middle East, Southeast Asia, total more than 40 countries and regions. During the past years, we have accumulated thousands of new and old customers. To resolve the subsequent consumable problems for carton manufactures, to figure out the shortcomings of the management and production and to support and improve the operation of carton manufacturers, we are integrating hundreds of suppliers to provide the overall, thorough and perfect services for them.

3.One-stop export services are available from the professional export salesmen.

4.The first company in China to provide the comprehensive consumables supplying for carton manufacturers.

5.The unique company in China with complete specifications of carton consumables.
