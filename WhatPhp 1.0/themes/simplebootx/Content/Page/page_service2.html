A. Technology

1.Production Process

Beginning with body paper, corrugated board is made through the process of high temperature, high pressure, roll compacting and gluing. After that, make it into the required cardboard sheet through the process of drying, grooving, slitting and trimming. Then by means of printing, slotting, die cutting, stapling or sticking box, make it into the required finished product of carton box.

2.Finished Product

The Carton box is made from corrugated board. The type of paperboard is divided into double wall (single sided single corrugated), triple wall (two-sided single corrugated), quintuple wall (two-sided two corrugated cored paper), septuple wall (two-sided three corrugated two cores paper) and so on.

3.Machine

The machine is manufactured by Shanghai Qisheng Packing Machinery Factory. We will supply the most advanced high quality corrugated paper board production line and print fluting machine.
